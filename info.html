Work

Eliza Darnell is an artist and graphic designer based in Virginia and Washington, DC. She recently completed her BFA at VCUarts in May 2023. She has a wide range of design interests, which include: brand identity, packaging, print & publication design, installation and exhibition design, editorial, web design, and art direction. Her style of work can range from clean and simple to eccentric and experimental, but is always synthesized into a thoughtful and sophisticated design.
